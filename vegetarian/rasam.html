Rasam

Rasam:
    Take tamarind size of lemon
    Start heating  tamarind water
    Add the following :
    Grind 2 tomatoes and add to that
    Asafoetida and salt
    Heat for 5 mts
    Add 2 spoons of rasam powder
    Heat for 5 mts
    Add dhal water
    Heat till bubbles form on top
    Add seasoning in ghee with mustard and jeera
    Add coriander leaves
