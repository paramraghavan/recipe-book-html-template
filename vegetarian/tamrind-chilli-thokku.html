Directions

    Splice the green chillies, such that they are still once piece
  1. Put mustard seed in sesame oil, once the mustard splits, then put split urad dal. Enough oil to cook chilies like we cook onion - no need to roast like onion
  2. Add sesame and methi to a pan and dry roast it, roast enough so you smell methi, don't make it black. Let this cool down. Grind this into powder
  3. Boil 2 cups water and add tamarind to it, once it cools down, squish the tamrind and strain the tamrind water
  4. Add to the above strained tamrind water to vessel
  5. Add turmeric, salt . Asofodeta and jaggery and cook well
  6. Once the above liquid becomes viscous, add the cooked chilies and cook it well.
  7. Now add the dry roasted and powdered methi and sesame, mix it well and cook for a minute
  8. Put out the flame, check salt level
  9. Cover the lid, keep it for 4 hours and transfer it to a glass botle.
  10. You are ready to serve
