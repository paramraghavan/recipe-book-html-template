Moong Dal

Info

Time
1:00 H
Difficulty
Servings
6

Ingredients

Step 1
 
1
cup whole moong dal
6
hours soak moong dal in water an drain it
2¼
cups water to drained moong dal, add to cooker
¼
teaspoon turmeric, add to cooker
½
teaspoon salt, add to cooker
Step 2
 
1
tablespoon ghee
⅕
teaspoon asafoetida/heeng
½
teaspoon jeera
¼
teaspoon turmeric
¼
teaspoon red chilli powder- as per taste, even optional
5
cloves, crush using mortar
9
peppercorns, crush using mortar
1
green chilli split half way
1
teaspoon coriander powder
¼
teaspoon garam masala
¼
cup milk - optional
2
inch ginger, grate it
1
big onion , grate it
3
medium sized tomatoes, puree/grind it

Directions

  1. Follow Step 1. Pressure cook on medium to high flame for 1 whistle. Then on low flame for 3 minutes. Wait for cooker to cool down before you open it.
  2. Follow ingredients in Step2. Heat the wok, add 1 table spoon ghee
  3. Add item from step2 into wok- heeng, jeera, chilli, coriander, cloves, pepper, split geen chilli, grated ginger.
  4. Add grated onion and add 1/2 table spoon salt. Cook all the items in the wok well, till it starts leaving oil.
  5. Add the pureed tomatoes, cook it well
  6. Add the prepared dal from Step 1
  7. boil the dal in high flame for 2-3 minutes
  8. Add the optional milk and give one boil
  9. Add garam masala and mix it well, give one quik boil. Garnish coriander
  10. Recipe reference
  11. You are ready to serve
