Mulaga Puli aka Tamrind Chilli

Mulaga Puli:
    - 1 cup puli
    - 3 cup split chilies
    - Asafodeta
    - Salt
    - Oil
    - Sml Amt jaggery
    - Turmeric
        Put mustard seed in oil, then put split urad dal. Enough oil to cook chilies like we cook onion - no need to roast like onion
        Put puli water, add turmeric, salt .
        Asofodeta and jaggery and cook well
    - 1/2 to 1 teaspoon methi and sesame seed, dry roast, powder it and add it at the end
